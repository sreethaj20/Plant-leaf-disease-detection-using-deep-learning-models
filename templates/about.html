About LeafyAI

Empowering farmers and gardeners with ML | DL | LLM's plant disease detection technology to improve crop health and productivity.

Our Mission

LeafyAI was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make advanced plant disease detection technology accessible to everyone. Early detection of plant diseases is critical for sustainable agriculture and food security worldwide.

By combining cutting-edge deep learning with user-friendly interfaces, we aim to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals identify and treat plant diseases efficiently, reducing crop losses and chemical use while increasing yields.

How LeafyAI Works

1

Upload Leaf Image

Simply take a photo of the affected plant leaf using your smartphone or camera and upload it to our secure platform. We support various image formats for your convenience.

2

ML | DL | LLM's Analysis

Our advanced deep learning model processes the image to identify patterns and symptoms associated with various leaf diseases. The model has been trained on thousands of labeled leaf disease images.

3

Get Results & Treatment

Receive detailed analysis of the detected disease, including severity assessment, treatment recommendations, and preventive measures to protect your plants and crops.

Deep Learning Model

Our core detection system uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on over 87,000 images of healthy and diseased plant leaves across 38 different classes. The model achieves a remarkable 96.5% accuracy in identifying plant diseases from images.

Technologies: TensorFlow, Keras, CNN architectures

LLM's Integration

Once a disease is identified, we use Multimodal LLM"s to provide comprehensive analysis and treatment recommendations. This gives users practical, expert-level advice on managing the detected plant disease with both organic and conventional approaches.

Technologies: LLM's Phi Agent, DuckDuckGo search integration
